About Jordan University of Science and Technology (JUST):

Jordan University of Science and Technology (JUST) is one of the leading academic and research institutions in the Middle East. Established in 1986, the university has earned a strong reputation for excellence in science, technology, engineering, and medicine. JUST's Faculty of Computer and Information Technology and the Faculty of Engineering are recognized for their rigorous academic programs, modern laboratories, and strong partnerships with international institutions and industry leaders. The university consistently promotes innovation, critical thinking, and hands-on learning, making it a top choice for students pursuing careers in technology and engineering. Its commitment to academic excellence and research-driven education prepares graduates to meet global challenges with confidence and creativity.
